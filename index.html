Phase Details

Phase 1 - Claude Knowledge Base & Notion CRM ▼

I will build a Claude Project that acts as the brain of your business. It will contain:

  • All your experience packages, pricing, and availability rules
  • FAQ responses in your brand voice - no generic AI tone
  • SOP documents: how to handle a new inquiry, a special request, a rescheduling
  • Itinerary templates for different traveler profiles (solo, couple, family, foodie, history)
  • Hidden Kyoto content library - your unique knowledge becomes queryable

Simultaneously, I will set up a Notion CRM with lead stages: New Inquiry → Qualified → Proposal Sent → Booked → Completed. Every lead is tracked, dated, and tagged automatically.

Phase 2 - Inquiry Automation (Instagram + WhatsApp) ▼

I will connect your inbound channels to Make.com, which routes messages to Claude for classification and response generation.

  • Instagram DMs: Instead of ManyChat, I will use respond.io - a purpose-built omnichannel inbox that supports Claude API natively. It delivers warm, personalised first responses within seconds.
  • WhatsApp: Connected via WhatsApp Business API through respond.io - same intelligent handling as Instagram.
  • Lead scoring: Claude categorises each inquiry (serious traveler vs. casual interest) and auto-tags in Notion so you know exactly who to prioritise.
  • Escalation logic: Complex requests are flagged for your personal reply - Claude never pretends to be you when it shouldn't.
Phase 3 - Follow-Up Sequences & Payment ▼
  • Automated follow-up emails at Day 2 and Day 5 if a lead goes cold - written in your voice, personalised by experience type
  • Claude generates a draft itinerary from the lead's details - you review and approve before sending
  • When you mark a lead as "Ready to Book" in Notion, a Stripe payment link is auto-sent via WhatsApp or email
  • Payment confirmation triggers the onboarding sequence automatically
Phase 4 - Onboarding, Post-Experience & Content ▼
  • Welcome pack auto-sent 5 days before the experience: what to wear, how to get there, what to expect
  • Day-before reminder with weather forecast, final logistics, and your personal note
  • Post-experience message 48 hours after: thank you + Google review ask + referral prompt
  • Instagram content workflow: Claude-assisted caption and hashtag generator, connected to Later or Buffer so you batch content once a week

05 - Workflow Documentation

8 Automated Workflows I Will Build

1. New Inquiry → Auto-Qualify → Log to CRM ▼
1

Message received (Instagram or WhatsApp)

respond.io captures the message and passes it to Make.com via webhook.

2

Claude classifies and responds

Make.com sends the message to Claude API with your knowledge base context. Claude generates a warm, branded first response and a lead score (hot / warm / cold).

3

Response sent + lead logged

Response is delivered via respond.io. Lead is created in Notion with name, channel, inquiry summary, and Claude's lead score. You see a clean dashboard - no inbox chaos.

2. AI-Assisted Itinerary Generation ▼
1

You move lead to "Proposal Stage" in Notion

This triggers a Make.com automation instantly.

2

Claude generates a custom itinerary draft

Using the lead's preferences pulled from Notion, Claude creates a full itinerary referencing your hidden Kyoto knowledge base. Traveler type, interests, pace, and dates are all factored in.

3

Draft lands in your Google Doc for review

You spend 5–10 minutes personalising - not 45 minutes writing from scratch. Then send.

3. Follow-Up Sequence (Cold Lead Recovery) ▼

If a lead hasn't responded in 48 hours, the system triggers a personalised follow-up in your voice, referencing their specific inquiry. A second follow-up goes at Day 5. If still no response, the lead is archived.

  • Day 2: Friendly check-in with a relevant detail about their inquiry
  • Day 5: Soft scarcity message - dates fill up, Claude references real availability logic
  • Day 10: Archived + tagged "Nurture" for future reactivation
4. Booking Confirmation + Stripe Payment ▼

When you mark a Notion lead as "Confirmed," Make.com auto-generates a Stripe payment link and sends it via WhatsApp or email - whichever channel the client used. On payment completion, Stripe triggers the onboarding workflow automatically. No manual steps, no missed payments.

5. Client Onboarding (Pre-Experience) ▼
  • T-5 days: Welcome pack email - experience overview, what to bring, meeting point map, your contact
  • T-1 day: Reminder with weather forecast, final logistics, and your personal note
  • Day-of: Morning check-in message via WhatsApp

All of this is auto-triggered by the booking date stored in Notion. You write the templates once, Claude personalises them per client.

6. Post-Experience Review + Referral ▼

48 hours after the experience date, a warm thank-you message is sent with a Google review link and a referral offer. If they leave a review, they are tagged in Notion as a potential testimonial - their network flagged as a warm referral audience for future Instagram content targeting.

7. FAQ Auto-Handler ▼

Claude handles the top 20 questions your business receives - pricing, availability, what's included, group sizes, accessibility, custom requests - without you lifting a finger. If a question falls outside the knowledge base, it flags it for your personal reply and suggests a draft for you to approve. The system self-improves as you add to the knowledge base over time.

8. Instagram Content Workflow ▼

Once a week, you spend 30 minutes in a content batching session. Claude generates 5–7 caption drafts with hashtag sets tailored to your hidden Kyoto niche. You edit, approve, and they're pushed to Later.com for scheduled publishing. Stories and engagement prompts are also templated - Claude pulls from your experience library to generate fresh hooks.
